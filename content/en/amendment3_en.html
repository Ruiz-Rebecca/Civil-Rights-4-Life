What does the 3rd Amendment state?

In the Bill of rights, the 3rd amendment is described as the following:

"No Soldier shall, in time of peace be quartered in any house, without the consent of the Owner, nor in time of war, but in a manner to be prescribed by law."

How does the 3rd amendment work as a civil right?

  • The Third Amendment gives citizens the right to refuse soldiers from being housed in their private homes without consent.
  • The 3rd amendment protects property rights
  • The 3rd amendment limits government/military power especially when there isn't war.
